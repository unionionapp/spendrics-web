Marketing has more data.
Less clarity.

Teams already have dashboards, exports, and channel reports everywhere. What they still struggle to see is where the money is actually working — and where it's silently draining away.

Measure Marketing ROI Across Channels

◎

Too Many Dashboards, No Decision Layer

Every platform reports its own metrics in its own format. There is no unified layer that translates signals into a clear budget decision — until now.

◎

Attribution That Misleads, Not Guides

Last-click and platform-reported attribution consistently overstate impact. Marketing teams end up over-investing in channels that look good on paper but underperform in reality.

Influencer budgets keep growing but measurement stays subjective. Teams need a deterministic way to score influencer impact against actual campaign outcomes.

HOW IT WORKS

From signal to decision.

Three steps. No black boxes. You always know why a score is what it is.

→
STEP 01

Ingest

We pull in signals from every platform your campaigns run on — ads, influencers, organic channels — and normalize them into a single stream. No more copy-pasting exports across five different dashboards.

→
STEP 02

Score

Each campaign gets scored on health, impact, efficiency, and confidence. The formulas are fixed and transparent — the same inputs always produce the same output. You can trace every number back to the data behind it.

STEP 03

Decide

You get a clear signal: scale this, fix that, stop funding the other thing. Active campaigns refresh every 15 minutes. Historical data updates daily. The decision is yours — Spendrics just makes it obvious.

PRODUCT PHILOSOPHY

Marketing Intelligence for Data-Driven Teams

Spendrics is built on three non-negotiable product principles. Every engine, every score, every recommendation must meet all three.

◎

Deterministic Analytics

Every score is computed from a defined, repeatable formula. The same inputs always produce the same outputs. Marketing teams can trust and verify every number Spendrics produces.

◉

Transparent Metrics

No black boxes. Every metric exposes its inputs, weights, and computation method. Teams understand not just what the score is, but exactly why it changed.

◈

Explainable Insights

Every recommendation comes with the evidence chain that produced it — so marketing leaders can present decisions to boards and stakeholders with full confidence and documented reasoning.
